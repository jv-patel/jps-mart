Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  We believe in fair and transparent refunds

At JPs Mart, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We offer a simple, fair refund and cancellation policy for all orders placed within Anand.

Cancellation Policy

✅ Before Order is Packed FULL REFUND

You can cancel your order for any reason and receive a full refund within 2–5 business days to your original payment method.

🟡 After Order is Packed but Before Dispatch PARTIAL REFUND

Cancellation is possible but a ₹20 handling fee will be deducted from your refund.

🔴 After Dispatch / Out for Delivery NO CANCELLATION

Once your order has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible. Please accept the delivery and raise a quality complaint if needed.

Refund Policy

Eligible for Full Refund

  • Wrong item delivered (different from what you ordered)
  • Item is damaged, spoiled, or of unacceptable quality
  • Item was missing from the order
  • Order not delivered within 30 minutes of placing

Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Incorrect address provided by the customer
  • Customer not available to receive the order
  • Perishable items opened and consumed

How to Raise a Refund Request

Contact us within 2 hours of delivery via WhatsApp with:

  • Your Order ID (shown in order confirmation)
  • A photo of the damaged/wrong/missing item
  • Your UPI ID for refund (if paid via UPI)

📱 WhatsApp: +91 90162 40490

Refund Timeline

  • UPI / Online: 2–5 business days
  • Cash on Delivery: Credited as JPs Mart wallet points (redeemable on next order)

COD Refunds

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are issued as JPs Mart loyalty points equal to the refund amount, credited to your account within 24 hours of approval. These can be redeemed on any future order.
