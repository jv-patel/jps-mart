Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025  |  Effective immediately

JPs Mart ("we", "us", or "our") operates the JPs Mart grocery delivery application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service in Anand, Gujarat.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone Number: Collected during login for OTP-based authentication via Firebase Phone Auth.
  • Delivery Address: Full address and pincode entered at checkout to fulfill your order.
  • Order History: Items purchased, amounts paid, and delivery details stored in Firestore.
  • Device Data: Browser type, operating system, and app usage patterns via Google Analytics (GA4).
  • Payment Intent: We do not store UPI transaction IDs or card details. Payments are processed via UPI directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your grocery orders within Anand.
  • To send order confirmation and status updates via WhatsApp.
  • To improve our product recommendations and app experience.
  • To send push notifications about your order status (with your permission).
  • To prevent fraud and maintain security of our platform.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase Firestore with industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Admin access is secured with hashed passwords and rate-limiting. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, analytics
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Anonymous usage tracking (G-EWGRZTWDRK)
  • WhatsApp Business API — Order confirmations (+919723415082)
  • Unsplash — Product images (no personal data shared)

5. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your account and order data by contacting us on WhatsApp.
  • Opt out of analytics tracking by using a privacy-focused browser.
  • Revoke push notification permissions from your browser settings at any time.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your cart, loyalty points, and preferences locally on your device. No cross-site tracking cookies are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
